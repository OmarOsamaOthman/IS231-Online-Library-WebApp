About Us



"Where the only effort left... is to read"

No more commutes, no more searching rows. Just open and explore.


Our Team

Our team consists of a group of experts in the field of libraries and software development. We work together to ensure that content is up to date and that features are developed that serve readers.

Our Goal

Our core goal is to provide a frictionless reading sanctuary. We envision a world where every book is instantly accessible, allowing you to dive into literature without ever stepping outside. It's about finding solace in the quiet comfort of your home, nestled among your own collection, perhaps with a hot cup of coffee by your side. We eliminate the need for travel or unwanted social interactions, placing the entire universe of knowledge directly into your hands.
